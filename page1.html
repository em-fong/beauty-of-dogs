German Shepherds

Fuzzy Defenders

German Shepherds are sweet, fluffy, and help you scare away unwanted solicitors who ring your doorbell. To many, this dog can be scary and intimidating, and it certainly earns its reputation as a hard-working of law enforcement and government agencies. But, this dog can also be the perfect family couch potato who loves to snuggle, eat, and mostly eat.
